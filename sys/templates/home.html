Objective

A generative term extraction method.

Combined with few-shot learning strategy and supervised fine-tuning.

Improves the text generation ability of GPT-3 for specific downstream tasks.

Accurately extract information science terms in low-resource labeled dataset scenarios.

Terms Distribution

Information Science terms in the full text of Journal of Informetrics, 2008-2021.

A total of 136,259 Information Science terms were extracted.

Method

Conducting Information Science terms recognition task on SSCI domain corpus.

1088 research papers, 51,273 paragraphs, 252,162 sentences, 6,074,778 words.

Model

  • NLU (BERT-like): BERT, SciBERT, RoBERTa
  • NLG (GPT-like): GPT-3 (davinci, curie)

Performance

  • Most accurate model: GPT-3 (F1: 87.96%)
  • Least accurate model: RoBERTa (F1: 79.40%)

Application

Automatic recognition of Information Science terms.

An easy to use platform.
